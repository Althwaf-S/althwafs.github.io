Who I Am

About Me

I am a final-year Aerospace Engineering student at Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan Engineering College, Perambalur (CGPA 8.29), with a demonstrated passion for aviation, space systems, and propulsion technology.

I have had the privilege of interning at ISRO's Satish Dhawan Space Centre (SDSC SHAR), Sriharikota — India's primary orbital launch facility — where I studied launch vehicle integration, spacecraft systems engineering, and mission control operations alongside ISRO's technical teams. This experience gave me firsthand exposure to rocket propulsion, launch pad operations, and aerospace safety protocols at the highest level.

My technical foundation spans aerodynamics, aircraft structures, flight mechanics, and rocket propulsion, reinforced by hands-on proficiency in SolidWorks, CATIA, AutoCAD, Fusion 360, and ANSYS. I have independently led projects ranging from RC surveillance UAVs and reusable launch vehicle prototypes to CubeSat telemetry systems and solid-propellant rocketry.

I am actively seeking full-time opportunities in aerospace engineering, aircraft manufacturing, propulsion systems design, or space technology — environments where I can contribute meaningfully from day one.
